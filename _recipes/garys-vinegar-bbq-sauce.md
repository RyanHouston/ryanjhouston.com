## Ingredients

  - 1 stick unsalted butter
  - 1/3 cup ketchup
  - 3 chopped jalapenos
  - 3 tbsp lemon juice
  - 2 tbsp honey
  - 2 tbsp dijon mustard
  - 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  - 2 tsp chile power
  - 2 tsp kosher salt
  - 1 tsp black pepper

## Directions

Mix all the above ingredients, heat to a boil, then remove from heat.

Add:

  - 1 cup apple cider vinegar
  - 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
